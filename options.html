Ganna Extension, to make your life easy.

Play/Pause Alt+Shift+P
Next Song Alt+Shift+N

Features

  • Keyboard Shortcut To Play & Pause
  • Notification Popup After Every Song Change
  • Popup Song Information
  • Opens Gaana.com on any Event (Keyboard Shortcuts/ Buttons in the Popup)
  • Saves Time For Searching Gaana.com Tab, for Pausing the Song
